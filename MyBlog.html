Sociology in Kazakhstan

The job market in Kazakhstan August 17, 2014

Sociology is quite fun discipline just entering Kazakhstani higher education and market. There are not many jobs for pure sociologists. A lot of such works can be occupied by people who do not have sociology education, but perhaps just humanitarian education or relative experience. This makes the job search hard and other skills very important.

Archaeological expeditions

Two fieldtrips I have gone to as a student September 16, 2014

Two expeditions were completely different both in nature and its practices. The first expedition was purely archaeological. I was living in Bayan-Zhurek valley, the mountainous area near Taldykorgan city, South-East Kazakhstan, for the archaeological summer school DMAP. The second expedition was a mix of archaeological and socio-cultural fieldtrip with elements of interview, and other interesting archaeological methods that I did not practice in the first.
