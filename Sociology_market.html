Have a focus

Sociology is quite fun discipline just entering Kazakhstani higher education and market. There are not many jobs for pure sociologists. A lot of such works can be occupied by people who do not have sociology education, but perhaps just humanitarian education or relative experience. This makes the job search hard and other skills very important.

You should dive into several disciplines of your interest within sociology.

In sociology, I love feminist thought, symbolic interactionism, socio-cultural sociology, ethnography.

I am not good at structural functionalism, political and economic knowledge.

I know what I am good at and am not. I dive deeper into my interests and improve my methodological and theoretical skills.

You should know too.
